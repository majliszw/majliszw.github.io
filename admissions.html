Admissions

  1. All Applications are FREE: Majlisul Ulama Zimbabwe does NOT charge for the submission of applications.
  2. No Guarantee of Acceptance: Submitting this application form does not guarantee acceptance to our institution.
  3. One Application per Student: Please submit this form only once for each student. Multiple submissions will not be considered.
  4. Application Deadline: The deadline for applications for the 2025 intake is Saturday, 31 August 2024. No applications will be accepted after this date.
  5. Application Review Process: Our Committee will review all applications, and only shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview.
  6. Honesty and Integrity: Any false or misleading information provided will result in the cancellation of the application.
  7. Islamic Values and Code of Conduct: Our institutions, IQRA DAARUL ILM and IQRA ACADEMY, are founded on Islamic principles and operate according to Islamic values. If accepted, students are expected to adhere to our Code of Conduct, rules, policies, and disciplinary measures as set by the Administration.


IQRA DAARUL ILM & IQRA ACADEMY

Before filling this form, confirm that you have clear pictures the following on your device (you will be required to upload them)

  1. Birth Certificate of the Student.
  2. Recent Passport size photo of the Student (taken not more than 6 months ago).
  3. Picture of the students latest academic school report - 2nd term 2024


CHILDREN'S HOME

Before filling this form, confirm that you have clear pictures the following on your device (you will be required to upload them)

  1. Birth Certificate of the Child.
  2. Recent Passport size photo of the Child (taken not more than 6 months ago).
  3. Death Certificate of Deceased Parents/s.
