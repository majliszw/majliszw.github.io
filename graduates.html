Anuual Graduates Seminar - December 2024

Deadline for applications is: Wednesday, 4th December 2024 @ 2pm.

Only for those who graduated in 2023 or earlier.

Only successful applicants will be issued a registration number via SMS & WhastApp.


Males | Anuual Graduates Seminar

The Anuual Graduates Seminar (Males) - will take place from Monday, 23 December 2024 - till Wednesday, 25 December 2024

  • Only for Graduates who have completed a FULL 1 Year Attacment.



Females | Anuual Graduates Seminar

The Anuual Graduates Seminar (Females) - will take place from Thursday, 26 December 2024 - till Saturday, 28 December 2024.

  • Only for Graduates who have completed the FULL course at the Girls Centre; this includes those who completed Form 6.
